Chapter6 - Access Control

convention

noun. 习俗；常规；惯例

the way in which sth is done that most people in a society expect and consider to be polite or the right way to do it

thornier

adj. (问题)棘手的，复杂难解的

If you describe a problem as thorny, you mean that it is very complicated and difficult to solve, and that people are often unwilling to discuss it.

rip

v. (突然或猛烈地）撕破，裂开

to tear sth or to become torn, often suddenly or violently

strait

noun. 尤指经济拮据引起的）困境，境况窘迫

a very difficult situation especially because of lack of money

cohesive

adj. 结成一个整体的

forming a united whole

tedious

adj. 冗长的；单调乏味的

lasting or taking too long and not interesting

insulate

v. 使隔热；使隔音

to protect sth with a material that prevents heat, sound, electricity, etc. from passing through

comprise

v. 包括；包含；由…组成

to have sb/sth as parts or members
